Code of Ethics

This is the ethical code that RodFireProductions strives to stand by with any and all actions that are taken and causes/others that are supported and colaborated with.

Expectations

Here it is implored and expected that one shows support, empathy, and hospitality to others regardless of:

  • Race or ethnicity
  • Sex
  • Gender identity or expression
  • Sexual or romantic orientation
  • Age
  • Ability
  • Marital or relationship status
  • Religion
  • Native lanuage
  • National origin or nationality
  • Geographic location
  • Socioeconomic status
  • Caste
  • Background

It is also expected of one to strive for further education when one is shown to be ignorant. Science is real.

Prohibited

It is prohibited for one to encourage, support, or engage in bigoted or prejudice purposes or action, including but not limited to:

  • Racism
  • Xenophobia
  • Islamophobia
  • Anti-semitism
  • Ableism
  • Pedophilia
  • Zooplilia and/or bestiality
  • Misogyny
  • Queerphobia
  • Queer exclusionism
  • Homophobia
  • Transphobia
  • Enbyphobia
  • Aphobia
